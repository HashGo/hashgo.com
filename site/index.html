#HowTwitterWasWon

HashGo builds apps and games that make the social commentary on Twitter more focused, and more fun.

Everyday hundreds of millions of users are expressing their opinion about celebrity scandal, sports rivalry and world news -- in one big unintelligible stream. We connect these armies of fans and haters so they can go head-to-head, keeping score on which of today's topics and trends are most loved and most despised. Everyone with an opinion now has a means - not just to comment - but to join sides and be counted in the arena of public opinion.

Our apps are a first of their kind, marrying monetizable F2P elements with "affinity-driven" user acquisition and the endless content-creation of each days' news cycle.

HashGo's flagship app TrendWars takes what's trending and places it on an interactive map. Any hashtag or at-handle is fair game. This includes political topics, brands, TV programs, athletes and more. The platform lets players direct their collective support or displeasure right at the source. This “attacking” and “defending” is done via the uber-popular game mechanic of “Match 3” (see Candy Crush / Puzzles & Dragons).

TrendWars mirrors Twitter in its real-time nature. When something trends on Twitter it shows up in the app. It also mirrors Twitter’s public, open format -- everyone is playing with (and against) everyone else. It's a cross between an intense water-cooler debate and a massive pillow fight. We just keep score.
